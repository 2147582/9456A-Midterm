About

Guitar Tutorial is a Web-Based guitar tutorial for beginners and professionals alike, that allows every individual to learn from the very basics of the guitar to the intermediate level of using the guitar. This website offers different exercises to sharpen up the user's guitar playing skills. The goal of the online Guitar Tutorial is to allow users to learn how to use the guitar easily and to also to give an opportunity for those who cannot afford lessons in music schools.
